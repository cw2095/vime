# Folding

Vimwiki allows you to use Vim's folding methods and options so you can fold your outline to make it less distracting and easier to navigate. You can use Vimwiki's built-in folding methods or supply custom methods for folding.  You control how folds behave with by setting the |g:vimwiki_folding| variable to the desired value in your configuration file: >
let g:vimwiki_folding = 'value'
